The Community We're Supporting

Our Mission

"Teach Them How to Fish"

The Southern Cameroon Spiritual and Relief Friendship Foundation (S.C.S.R.F.F.) is dedicated to supporting internally displaced persons in Akwaya subdivision, Cameroon. Our mission extends beyond immediate relief - we believe in empowering communities through education and sustainable support.

Currently, over 200 families have been displaced from their homes due to ongoing conflicts. These families, including countless children, have lost access to basic educational resources. Our Back to School initiative aims to provide essential school supplies, support teachers, and ensure that education continues even in the most challenging circumstances.

Every donation directly impacts a child's ability to learn, grow, and build a better future. Together, we can transform lives through the power of education and community support.
